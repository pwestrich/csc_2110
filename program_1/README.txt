Tic Tac Toe
by Philip Westrich
For CSC 2110 Assignment #1

To use the program:

1) Open the executable.
2) Enter the two player names. They must be one word, no spaces.
3) The game will randomly decide who goes first.
4) Enter your moves as prompted by the game.
5) Please don’t enter any invalid moves. It won’t let you.
6) The game will go until someone wins, or the board is full.

This game was written using Xcode 4.6 on Mac OS 10.8.2.
